## 3. Verification

1.  Add the images to the folders as listed above.
2.  Refresh the app.
3.  Go to **Strategies** > Click a strategy card > Click **Start Day** > View the list.
4.  If an image is missing, it will show a broken image icon. Check the filename spelling carefully!
